Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Opportunity Knocks

How are students selected for the platform?

Every student undergoes a thorough verification process to ensure they're currently enrolled students with the skills they claim. We partner with universities to identify promising talent across various disciplines.

What happens after I subscribe?

You'll gain immediate access to contact information for the student. From there, you can reach out directly to discuss internship details, expectations, and start dates.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely, you can cancel your monthly subscription at any time through your dashboard settings.

What if none of the matches are right for me?

Our algorithm continues learning from your preferences. If your initial matches aren't perfect, we'll refine your recommendations based on your feedback to find better candidates. You can use our filters to further refine your search.

What if I don't hear back from a company?

We encourage you to update your profile as you progress professionally to showcase new and refined abilities.

Are these internships paid?

We offer both paid and unpaid internships depending on what you are looking for.
